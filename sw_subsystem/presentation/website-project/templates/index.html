Machine Learning Tool for Detection and Prediction of Design Patterns in Quantum Software

Defining and using software patterns has proven to be key to developing quality software in a systematic way. These patterns are good solutions to common problems that developers encounter. There are a large number of design patterns in the literature that have been shown to work well and achieve their purpose.

On the other hand, with the rise of quantum computing, and the beginning of research in the field of quantum software engineering, it has been observed that there are still no well-documented and validated design patterns in the industry for quantum software, whose nature (radically different from traditional software) makes existing patterns invalid.

Because of this, this tool based on Machine Learning algorithms allows to analyze existing quantum software and detect or predict the use of certain proposed design patterns for quantum software.
